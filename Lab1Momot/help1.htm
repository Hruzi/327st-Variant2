Introduction

In this directory you can see how to use the program and what table fields are responsible for what


Stock ownership

It displays all the information about the ownership of shares, namely the account number, symbol, shares, purchase price, date of purchase

Account number

It indicates the ID of the account on which there are shares

Symbol

The symbol of the company owning this stock is displayed.

Stocks

Number of shares purchased by this company

Unit Price

Stocks

It displays complete information about each action, namely the industry, symbol, company name, price, maximum price, minimum price, ratio, beta, growth, price change, security, rating, rank, forecast, recommendations, risks. You can also select a symbol and click the display button to see the accounts that have these shares.

Industry

It displays complete information about the industry in the field of which companies with stocks work, Code, abbreviated name, full name. Here you can also select any industry code, click the display button and companies that work in this industry, their stock prices, etc. will appear below.
